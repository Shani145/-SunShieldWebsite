About Us

Sun Shield LLC is a distribution company that services its related companies and business partnerships across a diverse range of industries, including Plastics, Plastics Recycling, Contract Manufacturing, and Agricultural Soil Amendment Solutions. We also offer products and services related to Industrial Fans & MRO Equipment, Exercise Equipment, and provide comprehensive solutions for compliance, quality certifications, balance sheet consulting, transportation, and warehousing.

As a vertically integrated, full-service plastics business, Sun Shield LLC is actively involved in the distribution of both Virgin and Recycled Resin, Injection Molding, Compounding, Transportation and Warehousing, Toll Manufacturing, Contract Manufacturing, and Supply Chain Operations. Our audit and due diligence services cover all aspects of operational efficiency, while our Circular Economy Recycling solutions address the recycling of post-industrial plastics, supporting sustainable practices.

Our commitment to delivering value-added cost savings through a one-stop-shop model is tailored to meet the needs of our customers in fast-evolving markets. We have built a solid reputation as a solution-oriented partner, known for producing reliable, results-driven outcomes.
